About the app

Food filters make culinary photos more enticing. With a selection of over 20 filters optimized for food and drink photography, users can take exactly the type of food and drink photos they want. Brush gives license to users to create their own art and creative drawings that are integrated with the original image. Users can add own text message to original image. There are hundreds of different emojis and stickers that fit all tastes and occasions from business dinners to friend's nights out. The user can manually make the image brighter or darker, change the focus, and numerous other alterations to their taste when taking pictures. Sharing through a variety of services. Processed photos can be shared not only through WTX Hub but also on social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
